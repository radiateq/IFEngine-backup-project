                                                iforce2d Mod Matt • 7 years ago

                                                I think your step 1. should be enough to make a strong rope. The chain in step 2. does not need to be heavy, as it will not actually support the load.

                                                As long as you set the distance for the rope joint to be the same as the length of the chain of connected bodies, it should look correct when it stretches out to the maximum length. That is, to the player it will appear that the little bodies in the chain are causing the two endpoints to stay within the maximum distance, when it's actually the rope joint doing the work.

                                                There is an example of this in the testbed, look for the 'RopeJoint' test.
